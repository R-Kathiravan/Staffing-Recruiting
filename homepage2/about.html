Our Story

Founded in 2012 in the heart of Bengaluru, GeminiTalent started with a simple belief: traditional recruitment was broken. It was too slow, too transactional, and lacked the deep industry understanding required for high-stakes executive roles.

We set out to change that by combining the precision of data analytics with the intuition of seasoned headhunters. Today, we are the trusted partner for over 500 tech companies, from early-stage startups to Fortune 500 giants.

Our philosophy is simple: Quality over Quantity. We don't flood your inbox with resumes; we present you with your future leaders.
